Veltrax — Local‑First Platform

What is Veltrax?

Veltrax is a private, on‑premise AI platform. It connects your data, runs models locally, and shows live ROI.

  • Works with banking, retail, and legal data.
  • Runs on hardware you control.
  • Ships with real apps: Finex, Échelon, Verdict.

Why local‑first?

  • Privacy & control: data never leaves your building.
  • Cost & speed: no API bills; low latency on your LAN.
  • Proof: all actions are audit‑logged.

Details

  • Cloud & Local sources feed into DMZ Update Agent.
  • DMZ verifies and forwards to Veltrax Core.
  • Core processes and sends metrics to ROI & Audit.
